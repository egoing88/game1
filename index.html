About Caveman Adventure

Step into the shoes of a prehistoric hero in Caveman Adventure! Battle massive dinosaurs, gather food to survive, and claim your glory on the global leaderboard in this high-energy, classic 16-bit retro platformer.

🦕

Epic Challenges

Traverse through 10 increasingly difficult stages filled with wild prehistoric beasts, hazardous terrains, and complex platform pathways.

🍖

Survival Hunting

Collect food to keep your strength up! Swing your club on the ground or in mid-air to discover hidden food items scattered across the map.

🏆

Rank Mode Time-Attack

Put your speed-running skills to the test. Speed is king: clear stage 10 as fast as possible to climb the global leaderboard!

🏅

Perfect Clear Badge

Are you a true caveman champion? Complete all 10 stages without losing a single life to earn the prestigious PERFECT CLEAR badge next to your rank.

How to Play

← → Move Left / Right
↑ ↓ Climb Ladders / Look Up & Down
W Jump / Leap Over Obstacles
Spacebar Swing Club (Attack / Find Hidden Items)

💡 Prehistoric Pro-Tips:

  • Swing your club at suspicious empty areas—there might be delicious hidden meat or fruit that spawns from the ground or sky!
  • Timing is key: wait for dinosaurs to turn their back before closing in for the strike.
  • Avoid falling from high ledges, as landing on spikes or bottomless pits will cost you a life instantly.
